ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Thao and I’m an Atlanta based visual designer with a passion for creating beautiful products. I enjoy creating interaction designs that spark joyful experiences in an otherwise lacking interface. My skills include prototyping, micro-animation, and branding design. My goal isn’t to be the absolute best. My goal is to be what my industry needs the most which means to always be learning, improving, and collaborating. In my spare time, I enjoy illustration, jewelry-making, and being a mom to two wonderful fur babies.
